Learn to make amazing things!

SparkWaves teaches you how to make original and amazing things. Examples of things you will learn in this course are:

  • When to use a capacitor and how to choose one
  • What are the differences between transistors and when should you use each type
  • What is the difference between a voltage divider and regualtor
  • Much much more

This is not another guide on plugging an arduino together.
